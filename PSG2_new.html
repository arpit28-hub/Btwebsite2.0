What is the PSG GenicDocs - Document Management Software Grant?

In order to position Singapore as a global leader in technology, innovation, and entrepreneurship, the Productivity Solutions Grant (PSG) was established as part of the country's economic transformation plan. The PSG grant's main objective is to help companies and organisations use technological equipment and so lutions to enhance their operations.

Small- to medium-sized businesses (SMEs) from a variety of industries, including food, engineering, logistics, retail, and eCommerce, are covered by sector-specific solutions to promote the adoption of IT solutions, tools, and consulting services.
