Google Business Profile July Update 2024

Google has removed the calls and chat data from the performance tab, however, businesses can still download the data through Google Takeout.

In July 2024, Google Business Profile (GBP) introduced significant changes. Notably, the chat feature has been removed. This means businesses can no longer use GBP to chat with customers directly. However, customers can still find and contact businesses through Google Search and Maps. They can learn more from business descriptions, website links, and photos. This update aims to streamline the platform and focus on other contact methods.

Additionally, businesses should download records of past chats via Google Takeout before August 30, 2024. This ensures no loss of important communication data. Businesses are encouraged to use alternative chat channels for ongoing customer conversations. This update impacts all users of the chat feature.

How to Collect the Data of July Month of Calls and Messages?

Here are the steps to collect your data:

  • Visit Google Takeout: Go to Google Takeout.
  • Select Google Business Profile: In the list of available data, choose 'Google Business Profile'
  • Choose File Type, Frequency & Destination: Select your preferred file type, frequency, and destination for the export.
  • Create Export: Click 'Create export' to start the process.

By following these steps, businesses can efficiently download the old chat and calls of their google business profile.
