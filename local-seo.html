What Is Local SEO?

Local SEO helps businesses pop up in search results for places nearby. It works by tuning a website to show when people search for services close to them. It improves how often a business appears on Google Maps, local listings, and nearby searches. By using keywords tied to a location, businesses can draw in nearby customers, boost visits, and grow local sales. Local SEO in Singapore includes polishing the website’s content, building accurate business profiles, and collecting customer reviews. It’s key for businesses with a store or those serving nearby areas.

What are Local SEO Services?

Local SEO services are specialized strategies designed to enhance a business’s visibility in local search engine results. These services can be outsourced to an agency partner or local SEO consultant and often include:

Local Search Audit:

A local search audit can help to identify areas of your local SEO strategy that may need improvement.

Keyword Research:

Identifying and targeting the keywords that local customers are using to search for businesses like yours can help to improve your visibility in local search results.

Local Content Development:

Creating content that is relevant to the local area, such as local news, events, or guides, can help attract local customers.

Local Link Building:

Building relationships with other local businesses and websites can help to boost your visibility in local search results.

Google Business Profile Setup and Optimization:

A well-optimized Google Business Profile can significantly enhance your visibility in local search results.

Building and Managing Local Listings and Citations:

Citations, or mentions of your business on other websites, can help to boost your local search rankings. Then, ensuring that your business’s information is consistent across all citations can help to improve your local SEO.

How Local SEO is different from SEO?

Criteria SEO Local SEO
Target Audience Reaches a big audience, all over the country or world, without worrying about where people are. Focuses on people living close by, like in the same city or region.
Goal Aims to make websites rank higher on search engines, so they can attract people from everywhere. Helps businesses stand out in local search results, bringing nearby customers to the door.
Keyword Focus Picks common words related to the topic, that people across the world might search for. Local SEO chooses words that connect to a specific place, like city names, to catch local searchers.
Search Engines Works on many search engines, like Google and Bing, to improve a website's rank. Shines on Google, especially through Google My Business, and focuses on maps and local results.
Optimization Areas Boosts website content, and loading speed, and builds links from popular sites to make it more trustworthy. Sharpens business listings, and reviews, and enhances maps so local customers can find them easily.
Backlinks Gathers links from big websites and famous pages to raise a site's authority and trust. Local SEO services for small businesses collect links from local websites, like community blogs or newspapers, to strengthen the business locally.
Map Listings Focuses more on organic search results and doesn’t rely on maps. Local SEO services depend on Google Maps to highlight businesses in the area and guide customers to their location.
Business Info SEO doesn’t worry about the business's location, since it targets people everywhere. Local SEO needs detailed info, like the business’s address and phone number, to connect with local search results.

Why Google My Business is Important for Local SEO

Google My Business (GMB) guides your local customers to discover your business quickly. It positions your business where people can easily find it on Google or on maps. A complete GMB profile displays your business’s details, reviews, photos, and updates, building trust with your audience. It allows people to call you or visit your website instantly. GMB amplifies your presence by making sure local people notice your business first. With a full Google Business Profile optimisation, your business rises higher in search results, attracting more visitors.
