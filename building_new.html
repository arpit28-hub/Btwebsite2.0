Essential Link Building Terms To Know

When you use link building services in Singapore or try to create high-quality backlinks, it's helpful to know these important words. They will guide you through the process and make your website better for search engines.

1. Anchor Text:

Anchor text is the clickable part of a link. It tells people and search engines what the link is about. Choosing the right anchor text helps your site grow in white hat link-building.

2. Off Page SEO:

Off-page SEO includes tasks you do outside your website to help it rank higher in search engines. Getting backlinks from good websites makes your site stronger. You can boost your website by using off page SEO techniques with the help of a backlink building service.

3. DA/PA (Domain Authority & Page Authority):

DA and PA show how strong your website and its pages are. These numbers help you see how well your site may rank in search engines. You can use them in SEO backlink services to check the power of a link.

4. NAP (Name, Address, Phone Number):

NAP means your Name, Address, and Phone Number. Keeping the same NAP across different sites builds trust for your business. It helps make your off-page SEO stronger.

5. 404 Links:

404 links lead to pages that donâ€™t exist anymore. Fixing these links makes your website smoother for people to use. You can replace broken links with live links to improve your website and help with page optimization in digital marketing.

6. Live Links:

Live links are working links that point to your website. They bring more people to your site and help it grow. A backlink service gives you live links to boost your ranking in search results.

7. Spam Score:

Spam Score tells if a website could be doing something wrong. Keeping a low spam score protects your website and makes it trustworthy. It is important for white hat link-building.

8. Referral Traffic:

Referral traffic happens when people visit your site by clicking a link from another website. This helps your website grow and shows that your off page SEO is working well. More visitors to your site mean more success for your business.
